Fort Lake

Fort Lake is situated right in front of the Durga Temple entrance of the Belgaum Fort, Location: VG8H+43C, Shivaji Nagar, Belagavi, Karnataka 590016

OverView

Fort Lake, also known as Kote Kere, is a popular picnic spot in Belgaum City. Located right in front of the Durga Temple entrance to Belgaum Fort, the lake offers a serene environment surrounded by lush greenery. Walkways along the lake's boundary attract visitors who enjoy peaceful strolls, especially in the evenings when the sunset casts a golden glow over the calm waters. The lake features a variety of attractions, including pedal boats and floating restaurants, offering visitors unique experiences on the water. On weekends, a fountain show lights up the atmosphere from 6 PM to 8 PM, drawing crowds from across the city. Food stalls around the lake serve delicious street food, adding to the lively vibe. For families, a mini amusement park inside the lake premises provides entertainment for children. Open daily from 6 AM to 8 PM, Fort Lake is a delightful destination for relaxation and recreation.
